The *Hands-on Lattice Calculations in Python* course is meant to present you a series of "conceptual" exercises to help understanding the basics of transverse beam dynamics. 

The course material is available on [github](https://github.com/cerncas/hands-on-lattice-exercises). 

Contributed to the following schools:
- CAS 2024/09-10, *Introduction to Accelerator Physics*, Santa Susana, Spain
- CAS 2023/09-10, *Introduction to Accelerator Physics*, Santa Susana, Spain
